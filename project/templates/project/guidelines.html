How SpamShield Email Detection Works

Connect your Gmail account, link your Telegram, and receive real-time notifications whenever spam or non-spam emails are detected in your inbox.

Step-by-Step Setup Guide

What You’ll Need

  • A valid email ID to log in
  • Access to your Gmail account
  • Telegram installed on your phone

What You’ll Get

  • Automatic scanning of your emails
  • Spam alerts on your Telegram
  • Clear view of spam and non-spam emails
Complete all 8 steps once to activate alerts
1

Step 1 – Login Requirement

Users must first log in to the system using a valid email ID. This links your SpamShield account to your email so that all future spam detection and notifications are personalized for you.
Secure account-based access
2

Step 2 – Connect Gmail

After logging in, go to the Email section of SpamShield and click the "Connect Gmail" button. This authorizes the system to securely read your emails so that spam detection can be performed.
Gmail account connection Click “Connect Gmail”
3

Step 3 – Successful Connection

After clicking the button, the system displays a success message confirming that Gmail is connected. Once you see this confirmation, your inbox is ready to be scanned by the Email Spam Detection system.
Look for the success message
4

Step 4 – Get Telegram Chat ID

Open the Telegram app on your mobile phone and search for the bot named "ID BOT". Start a chat with this bot and send the command /getid. The bot will reply with your unique Telegram Chat ID.
Search for “ID BOT” Send /getid command
5

Step 5 – Submit Chat ID

Copy the Chat ID you received from Telegram and paste it into the field provided in the system. Submitting this Chat ID allows SpamShield to send spam detection results directly to your Telegram account.
Paste Chat ID into SpamShield
6

Step 6 – Request Processing

After submitting the Chat ID, the system sends a request to process your connected Gmail inbox. During this phase, you simply need to wait while the Email Spam Detection system analyzes your emails in the background.
Please wait while processing completes
7

Step 7 – Spam Detection Results

Once the system finishes processing your emails, the spam detection results are sent through the Telegram bot named "Spam detector". This bot reports which emails in your inbox have been classified as spam and which are identified as non-spam.
Results via “Spam detector” bot
8

Step 8 – Telegram Notifications

You will receive notifications on your mobile phone through Telegram indicating:
  • Spam emails detected in your inbox
  • Non-spam emails that are safe
Instant alerts on your phone
Start Email Spam Detection

Complete all the steps once to fully activate your Email Spam Detection workflow. After setup, SpamShield continues to monitor your inbox and keep you informed via secure Telegram notifications.
