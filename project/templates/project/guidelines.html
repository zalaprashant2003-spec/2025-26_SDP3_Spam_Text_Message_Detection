How SpamShield Works

Follow these simple steps to connect your email, link Telegram, and receive real-time spam detection alerts.

Login First See Steps

Step-by-Step Setup Guide

1. Login Requirement

To use SpamShield, you must first log in with a valid email ID. This ensures that your settings and alerts are linked to your account.

2. Connect Gmail

After logging in, navigate to the Email section and click the Connect Gmail button to authorize access to your inbox for spam scanning.

3. Successful Connection

Once connected, the system will show a success message confirming that Gmail is linked and ready for spam analysis.

4. Get Telegram Chat ID

Open Telegram and search for the bot named ID BOT. Send the command /getid and the bot will reply with your Chat ID.

5. Submit Chat ID

Copy your Chat ID from Telegram and paste it into the field provided in the system. This enables the bot to send alerts directly to you.

6. Request Processing

After submitting the Chat ID, SpamShield sends a request to process your inbox. Please wait a short while while the system analyzes incoming emails.

7. Spam Detection Results

When processing completes, results are sent through the Telegram bot named Spam detector. The bot will list emails detected as spam and not spam.

8. Telegram Notifications

You will receive Telegram notifications on your phone indicating:

  • Spam emails detected in your inbox
  • Non-spam emails confirmed safe
